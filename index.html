Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I add properties to my route?

Go to the **Properties** tab, tap **"Add Property,"** and enter the property details. Once added, they will appear in your route planner.

2. How does route optimization work?

When you add multiple properties, the app calculates the **most efficient route** based on location and travel time.

3. Can I save my routes for later?

Yes! Your entered addresses, ratings, and notes are saved within the app for easy access.

4. Does ShowNGo track my location?

No, **ShowNGo does not store or share any location data**. Your location is only used in real-time for route calculations.
